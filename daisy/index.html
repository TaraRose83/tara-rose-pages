Ten years, not a fresh start

I did not arrive at Tara Rose last year. I have been inside it for ten. I learned the method here, I watched it look after thousands of women, and for three of those years I was a Manager of the Year finalist. So when I say Bahrain is the same Tara Rose, I am not repeating a line from a brochure. I lived it.

The founding cohort is how we start it together. The first forty-five build this with me, and I will know every one of them by name.
