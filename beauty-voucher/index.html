Questions

The honest answers.

Is this money off?
No. It is your own plan, paid forward. You set money aside and we add more credit on top, so every visit you were always going to make is worth more.
How does the credit work?
It spends like cash on your plan, colour, cuts, treatments and home care, across any of our salons. Once it is on your account it stays yours until it is used or the validity date passes. It is not clawed back and not conditional.
Is there any small print at the till?
No surprises. Larger colour work is covered up to a set amount per visit, with any remainder settled in the usual way. Your stylist tells you before, never at the till. The credit moves with you between our salons, because the plan is the same at every one.
Can I split the payment?
Yes, with Tabby in the UAE, at no extra cost to you. Same credit, same bonus, paid your way. It is a kinder path to the same result, not a finance product.
Can I give it as a gift?
Yes. The voucher is sent in her name, ready to book, with a note from you. A gift of being looked after, not another thing she did not ask for.
What if I miss this season’s window?
The window reopens next quarter and the value returns with it. Leave us your details and we will let you know when it opens, so you have not missed your only chance, just this season’s.
