How it works

One partner per category. First to say yes holds it.

  • We place one brand per category beside our launch. One wellness studio. One aesthetics clinic. One fashion or lifestyle label. One members’ club.
  • Once a category is taken, it is closed for twelve months. We will not place a second brand of that kind beside us, and we will tell any later enquiry, honestly, that the category is held.
  • When the twelve months are up, the partner nearest that category is invited first to continue, before it reopens to anyone else.
This is real. If your category is already held, we will tell you, and we will tell you when it frees up. We would rather lose you honestly than keep you loosely.
